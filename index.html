Creating a Live Page

Step 1

First, you will select "create new repository". Once you get to that page, you will create a name then select "initialize this repository with a README.

Step 2

After you create your repository, its time to upload your files. Select the upload files option.

Step 3

Select and drag your index.html, css, and assets file all at once. Select "commit changes".

Step 4

After you upload your files, go to settings in the top right in the toolbar underneath your repository name.

Step 5

Once you get to the settings, underneath Github pages under Source, select "master branch". It will refresh the page and underneath the Pages it will be blue which means its loading your link.

Step 6

Refresh your page again and scroll down to Github pages. The bar should now be green with your site url. Click the url and it will take you to your now live page.
